Welcome to the Era of Superabundance

Exploring the Dawn of Unprecedented Prosperity

March 31, 2024

Since the beginning of human civilization, our existence has been shaped by scarcity—of resources, time, and opportunity. Now, we stand in a profound transformation in human history. New technologies are fundamentally reshaping how we live, work and create value. Enter the era of superabundance.

Powering the Superabundance Economy

Energy becomes so cheap it basically is free, now let's consider what this would cause in our current economy.

Manufacturing: Production costs plummet across industry, potentially enabling near zero marginal cost in what is becoming an almost fully robotic autonomous manufacturing.

Logistics: transportation expenses would dramatically decrease, reshaping the global robotic autonomous supply chain.

Agriculture: food becomes almost free, as desalination and water purification become economical, labor goes close to zero as autonomous robots harvest.

Resource Extraction: Mining shifts from fossil fuels to renewable materials. Energy-intensive processing becomes viable. Autonomous robots extract continuously. Environmental remediation becomes standard practice.

Compute: Data centers operate at maximum capacity without energy constraints. AI processing becomes ubiquitous. Quantum computing accelerates breakthroughs. Global digital infrastructure expands to all regions.

Education: Learning becomes personalized and lifelong. AI tutors adapt to individual needs. Virtual reality enables immersive experiences. Global knowledge access eliminates educational inequality.

Healthcare: Bioprinting creates organs on demand. AI diagnostics prevent disease. Medical robots perform precision surgeries. Personalized treatments become universally accessible.

Construction: 3D-printed buildings rise in days not months. Self-healing materials reduce maintenance. Carbon-negative structures absorb emissions. Adaptive architecture responds to environmental conditions.

And finally…

Creative Industries: AI collaboration tools amplify human creativity. Virtual production eliminates physical constraints. Creator economy flourishes with minimal barriers, the personal brand, and audience amplification becomes a form of value and wealth. Digital experiences become indistinguishable from reality, the simulation theory.

Human Activity in the Superabundance Economy

People will shift from a service-based economy to a meaning-based economy. Value creation resources include: human creativity, experience design and curation, relationship cultivation, purpose-driven ventures, ethics and culture.

Unlike the service-based economy which monetizes on energy and performing tasks/labor, the new economy would value only uniquely human capabilities that a machine cannot replicate - particularly those involving wisdom, context, empathy and imagination.

The concept of companies will change completely, and focus on human potential acceleration, helping humans discover purpose and develop skills. These organizations will excel when connecting people with purpose. As material scarcity diminishes, winning businesses will help people navigate questions of meaning, identity, and fulfillment.

As we move into this era, our jobs will become very much so of supervision, approval and storytelling.

The most crucial skills of the 21st century aren't technical but philosophical—critical thinking, empathy, and adaptability.

All this has been said before, in one way or another, but I truly believe that this change is happening in the next 5 years. I'm personally excited to be designing the future with intention of being intelligent, autonomous and deeply human-centered.
